## Retro Assessment

Our team used the Start/Stop/Continue retro outline. Each team member contributed ideas based on what they believed the team should start doing, stop doing, and continue doing. We then discussed these ideas together and selected several actionable improvements to focus on during the next sprint.

Assessment: The retro was productive and helped highlight several workflow improvements, particularly around testing, PR reviews, and task organization.

Advice: Future retro leaders should ensure everyone has the opportunity to contribute ideas and help prioritize the most impactful improvements for the team.

## Experiment/Change

Description: For this sprint, the team decided to experiment with clearer PR deadlines, stronger PR review practices, and better issue tracking before assigning work.

Assessment: These changes helped improve workflow clarity and reduced last-minute stress during development.

Decision Going Forward: The team will continue using structured issue tracking, earlier PR deadlines, and stronger code review practices in future sprints.
