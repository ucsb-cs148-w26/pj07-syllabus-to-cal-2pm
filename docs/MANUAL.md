### **Description of Product Purpose**

Plannr parses students' syllabi in order to convert them into Google Calendar events, making calendar organization more efficient and automated for users.

### **Intended User Audience**

Our intended user audience is college and high-school students who are juggling multiple classes and syllabi. This app is designed to streamline the process of figuring out when certain assignments are due, when exams are scheduled, and more.

### **Features**

#### Add Classes

Users can add multiple classes/syllabi and view their class list on the home page.

#### Upload Syllabi

Users can upload their syllabi in multiple ways, including PDF upload, camera scan, and text upload.
